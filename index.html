What is Lord of the Mysteries(LOTM)? Well it's a Chinese web novel written by Cuttlefish That Loves Diving. It is a great series in my opinion with about 1432 chapter along with its not so great sequel Circle Of Inevitability. Yes it is long but it is amazing. Please don't read it in 1 sitting if you do. That is the #1 reason why people stop reading it from what I've seen. Please note that this site will have spoilers. I have spoiler tagged them though in the off-chance a viewer actually reads LOTM .

What is it about?

Well LOTM is well an isekai series where Chinese Zhou Mingrui gets transmigrated into steampunk Britain. This series focuses around supernatural aspects and has quite the amount of Cthulu like mythology. (I have no clue how to refer to it by ngl). The power system works around 22 pathways and 9 sequences for each one. You could think of a pathway as a class in a game and sequences being levels. The sequences start from Sequence 9 and go all the way up to Sequence 1. However to go through each sequence you need to drink a potion. These potions are made up of various mystical creatures, plants, objects (for one of the pathways you have to eat your PHD thesis 💀). As you progress each potion's ingredients will get harder to acquire.
